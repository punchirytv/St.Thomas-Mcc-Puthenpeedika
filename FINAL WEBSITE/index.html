Welcome to St. Thomas Malankara Catholic Church

Benziger Reverend Hon. Achan was accepted and sent to Mannanath Ashram as per Achan's wish for Taksa training. Immediately Hon. At Achan's place (where the Guardian Angels' Church now stands At the place) the work of the chapel also started. At the end of June Hon. Achan was made a member of the Triusabhar by Benzigar Tirumeni in Kollam and KM Varughese Vakiel in the East, Kochu Koshi Vaidyan in Thannimoot, Nainan Varghese in the West, Baihoot Chacko, Mutirakalail Ikka, Vazhetil Koshi, Plamkootil Chacko and Mamootil Chacko also took oath and joined the Catholic Church. Lawrence Perera came and stayed at the chapel and accepted 120 people into the Catholic Church from here. And the church in the name of guardian angels within six months Finished building. Hon. Achan also started working as an assistant vicar. His life goal was to preach the Vedas. For that he endured many hardships and worked in many places. It was with the reunification of his father that religious preaching meetings were started in Madhya Thiruvithamkur. He had a prominent position among learned and eloquent speakers.Many conferences were held in many places. It can be said that no conference has been held in any place without Geeverugees Achan in Hon.Pitika. of the Antiochian Wreath Movement His missionary activity increased with the rise.
